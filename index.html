Inspire • Grow • Yield • Motivate

Bringing young people, earth learning, and meaningful outreach into one shared movement.

The International Geoscience Youth Movement creates spaces where curiosity becomes action. Through immersive programs, field experiences, and community-led engagement, IGYM invites young people to learn with humility, lead with purpose, and stay connected to people and place.

Current Pathways

  • Prayoga 2025 explores experiential learning through reflection, collaboration, and field-based participation.
  • Himalayan Outreach centers service, relationship-building, and place-based engagement in mountain communities.
  • Prayoga 2026 carries the movement forward with a new cycle of learning, dialogue, and involvement.

What We Do

Programs shaped by experience, reflection, and grounded community connection.

Immersive Learning

Programs that move beyond classrooms and encourage participants to learn through encounter, observation, and lived experience.

Community Engagement

Outreach initiatives that value long-term relationships, local context, and thoughtful participation over performative action.

Youth Leadership

Opportunities for young people to step forward with responsibility, imagination, and a stronger sense of collective purpose.
