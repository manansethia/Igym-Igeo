About IGYM

A movement rooted in earth learning, youth participation, and meaningful service.

IGYM brings together young people, place-based learning, and community-centered action through programs that are reflective, immersive, and deeply human.

Our Mission

IGYM exists to create transformative opportunities for young people to engage with landscapes, communities, ideas, and each other in ways that foster responsibility, curiosity, and long-term care. We see learning not as information alone, but as a process of relationship and encounter.

Our Approach

Our programs are built around immersion, dialogue, and participation. Whether through outreach, field experience, or collaborative learning spaces, we aim to help participants grow not only in knowledge, but in perspective, empathy, and purpose.
